Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

a class or small school for young children, usually between the ages of four and six to prepare them for primary education.

Elementary School

Elementary school age is typically between 5 and 11 years old, depending on the school system. Elementary school is primary education.

Middle (10-16) Years

Middle school age is typically between 11 and 14 years old.Middle school is school for children in the mid stages of adolescence.
